To start zookeeper server:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\zookeeper-server-start.bat config\zookeeper.properties

To start kafka server:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-server-start.bat config\server.properties

To create topic:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --create --topic demo-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 1

To create topic with retention:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --create --topic demo-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --replication-factor 1 --partitions 3 --config retention.ms=3600000

To produce data from console:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-console-producer.bat --topic demo-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092

To consume data from console:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-console-consumer.bat --topic demo-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092

To consume data from console from the beginning:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-console-consumer.bat --topic demo-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --from-beginning

To consume data from console with partion wise:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-console-consumer.bat --topic demo-topic --partition 2 --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --from-beginning

To get list of topics:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --list

To delete the topic:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --delete --topic demo-topic 

To describe the topic:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-topics.bat --bootstrap-server localhost:9092 --describe --topic order-topic

To alter cleanup policy of particular topic:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-configs.bat --alter --entity-type topics --entity-name order-topic --add-config cleanup.policy=compact --bootstrap-server localhost:9092

To describe the kafka configs of particular topic:
D:\Softwares\kafka>.\bin\windows\kafka-configs.bat --describe --entity-type topics --entity-name order-topic --bootstrap-server localhost:9092

To stop zookeeper server:
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\zookeeper-server-stop.bat config\zookeeper.properties

To stop kafka server
D:\Softwares\kafka> .\bin\windows\kafka-server-stop.bat config\server.properties
